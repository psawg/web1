Grand Theft Auto VI

About Game

Grand Theft Auto VI is an upcoming electronic game in the action-adventure genre developed by Rockstar Games. This is the eighth original installment in the Grand Theft Auto game series, following Grand Theft Auto V (2013), and the sixteenth overall in the franchise. Set in the fictional state of Leonida (based on Florida), the story will revolve around the criminal duo Lucia and her accomplices.

Genres: Action game, Open world, Nonlinear gameplay
